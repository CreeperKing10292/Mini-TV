Brief overview

Mini TV is a small, plug-in media player: boot straight into a clean minimal system, browse a local library present directly on the Raspberry Pi, and play songs/videos. No cloud. No distractions.

Speaker-first
Enclosure volume goes to sound. The screen stays small and purposeful.
2" TFT
Track info, video playback, and navigation—made for tiny pixels.
Plugged-in
Designed to live on a desk using USB 5V power.

Minimal design

A calm rectangle. A small screen window. A clean speaker face. Buttons are only what you need.

  • Front: speaker cutout + TFT window
  • Side: power + push buttons
  • subtle venting and a handy cable path
